AI Literacy at Work

Knowing how AI works, how it "learns", where the limits can be found are important skills to live in a world with AI.

This project is about measuring so-called AI Literacy, a construct that can tell us how competent people are regarding AI. We also look into how the outer appearance of AI influences a users perception of it.

More about the project can be found on our project website.

We also developed VR application showing how we might work together with AI in the future. These are implemented using Unity, the Oculus Integration Toolkit, and Microsoft Azure Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech, so that our AI representations can talk to the users. These applications were shown on June 16th, 2023 in the Bundesministerium für Arbeit und Soziales and also on the 12.09.2023 for the opening of the Fraunhofer KI Studios in Munich (see on the right) .
